Our Story

Passion on a Plate,
Every Single Day

Central Café was born from a simple belief: that great food, warm service, and beautiful surroundings can turn any meal into a memory. Nestled in the heart of Carvoeiro — one of the Algarve's most beloved coastal villages — we blend Portuguese tradition with international flair.

Our kitchen runs on locally sourced produce, certified free-range Black Pig, and Atlantic seafood delivered fresh daily. Every flame-grilled steak, every handcrafted burger, every plate of pasta carries the same commitment — the finest ingredients, treated with care.

Whether you're enjoying breakfast on the terrace or gathering with family over a long Sunday lunch, Central Café is your home away from home on the Algarve coast.
